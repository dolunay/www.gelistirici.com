Why we created Syrna

We launched this project to address the need for a flexible, extensible, manageable, sustainable, and compatible management information system. The XMS concept is essentially a platform. All processes, from software production to operation, are conducted using online techniques.

Our Story

Development work began in 2003 to address the need to code and manage the Kenthaber portal. Subsequently, the ERP product, now in production, continued to expand its scope and capabilities.

What we do

Syrna Generator, Syrna ERP, Syrna Framework, and Syrna management procedures provide a turnkey solution. The standard coding structure and the ability to develop without requiring any coding knowledge ensure extremely low post-installation maintenance and operating costs.
